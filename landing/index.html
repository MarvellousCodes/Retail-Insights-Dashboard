Premium Feature

Invoice Scanner

Every time a delivery arrives, your staff spend hours going through sheets of barcodes, manually checking what is already on the system, what is missing, and what has changed. RetailGuard's Invoice Scanner does all of that in seconds.

  • Scan the invoice, not every barcode Upload or photograph a supplier invoice and RetailGuard reads every product line automatically.
  • Instant match against your live stock See exactly which products are already in your system, which are new, and which have a price discrepancy.
  • Add missing products in one click New items on the invoice get flagged immediately. Approve them straight from the scanner. No manual data entry.
  • Catch cost price changes automatically If a supplier has changed a price on their invoice, RetailGuard flags it so you can update your margin before the product hits the shelf.
