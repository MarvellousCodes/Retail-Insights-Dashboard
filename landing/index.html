Common questions

Do I need new hardware?

No. RetailGuard works with your existing CBE or Pervasive POS. No new till, no new scanner, no IT changes.

Is my data safe?

Yes. The system is triple-locked read-only, so it can query but never modify, export, or delete. Data stays in Ireland, fully GDPR-compliant.

How much does it cost to run?

Pennies. Ask Your Shop costs ~€0.001 per question, Invoice Scanner ~€0.001 per scan. Hard monthly ceiling means no surprise bills. Total running cost: ~€1/month.

How long does setup take?

One afternoon. Export your existing POS data (you already do this), send it to us, and your dashboard is live the same day.

Works with my symbol group?

Yes. Fully compatible with GALA, BWG, and Musgrave supply chains. We already track 38,790 GALA products.

What if I just want to try it?
