RetailGuard Landing Gallery

Production 3D batch: five real marketing pages, each with exactly one restrained 3D moment that serves the retail story. Every page degrades to a static image on mobile and under reduced motion, caps pixel ratio, and pauses when offscreen. All copy rules enforced, forms live and tagged per page, illustrative numbers only.
