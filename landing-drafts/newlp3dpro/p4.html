Frequently asked questions

What till systems do you support?
We connect to all major EPOS systems used in Irish and UK convenience stores, including CBE, Henderson Technology, and Symbol Group platforms.
How long does setup take?
Most shops are live within 48 hours. We handle the data connection. You do not need to install hardware or change your workflow.
Is my data safe?
Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Only you can access your shop's information. We never share data between retailers.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly billing, no contract, no cancellation fee. If RetailGuard is not finding value, you can leave at any time.
Do I need to change how I work?
No. RetailGuard reads your existing data. You keep using your till, your suppliers, and your processes exactly as they are.
What kind of savings can I expect?
Typical opportunities identified can amount to thousands of euros per year, depending on store size. Results vary based on product range and current pricing practices.
