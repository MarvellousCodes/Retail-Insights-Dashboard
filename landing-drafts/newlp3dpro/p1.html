Frequently asked questions

What till systems do you support?
We connect to all major EPOS and back-office systems used by independent retailers in Ireland and the UK, including CBE, Epos Now, and proprietary symbol group systems.
How long does setup take?
Most shops are live within a day. We handle the data connection so you do not need to install anything on your till.
Can RetailGuard change my prices automatically?
Not without your say-so. RetailGuard shows you the opportunities and gives you the tools to act. Any price change is always your decision.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, stored on EU servers, and never shared with anyone. You own your data fully.
What if I want to cancel?
Cancel any time with 30 days notice. No exit fees, no lock-in. We keep it simple.
Do I need a long contract?
No. All plans are month to month. No annual commitment required.
