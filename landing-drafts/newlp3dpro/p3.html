Questions and answers

What till systems do you support?
We work with most modern EPOS and back-office systems used by Irish convenience stores and forecourts. If you are unsure, book a free scan and we will confirm compatibility.
How long does setup take?
Typically one day. We connect to your existing data export. There is no new hardware to install and no disruption to your trading day.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your figures with anyone, including other RetailGuard customers. Each shop is completely isolated.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes. Monthly billing, no lock-in. Cancel whenever you like and your data is deleted within 30 days on request.
What if I have multiple shops?
The Multi-site plan covers all your locations with a single dashboard. Each shop gets its own data and per-site onboarding.
Do I need to change how I work?
No. RetailGuard reads from your system, it does not change it. You keep using your till and suppliers as normal. We just show you what your data already says.
