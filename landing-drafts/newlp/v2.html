Common questions

Does it change anything on my till system?
No. RetailGuard connects read only. It cannot modify prices, stock levels, or any settings on your system. Your data stays exactly as it is.
How long does setup take?
We handle everything. A short remote session to connect, then your first results are ready within 48 hours. No hardware, no software to install on your end.
What till systems work with RetailGuard?
We currently support CBE and Touchstore (RS Back Office / Pervasive). If you use a different system, get in touch and we will confirm compatibility.
Can I try before I commit?
Yes. The free margin scan gives you a full report on your current stock, with no obligation. You see what RetailGuard finds before you decide.
Is my data safe?
Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Only you and your team can access it. We never share data between customers.
What if I have multiple shops?
The Multi-site plan covers all your locations with per-site breakdowns and cross-shop comparisons, so you can spot which site needs attention first.
