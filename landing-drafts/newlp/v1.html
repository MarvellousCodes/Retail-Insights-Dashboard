Common questions

Will this change anything on my till system?

No. We use a read only connection. Your till system, your prices, and your records stay exactly as they are. We only look at the data.

How long does setup take?

Most shops are live within 3 to 5 working days. We handle the technical setup remotely. You do not need to install anything or change your workflow.

What till systems do you support?

We currently work with CBE, Henderson Technology, and most Epos systems used by Irish convenience shops. If you are unsure, get in touch and we will confirm.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes. Monthly rolling, no lock in. Cancel with 30 days notice and your data is securely deleted.

Is my data safe?

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, stored on EU servers, and only accessible by you. We never share your data with anyone.

Do I need to be technical?

Not at all. The dashboard is designed for shop owners who do not use spreadsheets. Ask questions in your own words and get plain answers.
