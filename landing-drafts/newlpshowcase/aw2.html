Questions

What till systems do you support?
We connect to most EPOS systems used by Irish and UK convenience stores, forecourts, and independent retailers. If your system exports sales data, we can work with it.
How long does setup take?
One remote session, usually under an hour. We handle the connection to your existing system with no new hardware needed.
Can RetailGuard change my prices?
RetailGuard is read-only by default. It shows you what needs attention. You decide what to change, when.
What if I only have one store?
The Insight or Complete plan is designed exactly for single-site operators. Multi-site is there when you grow.
Is there a free trial?
We offer a free margin scan so you can see the value before committing. No card required, no obligation.
