Frequently asked questions

What till systems do you work with?
We connect to most major EPOS and back-office systems used in Irish and UK convenience retail. If your system exports sales data, we can work with it.
How long does setup take?
Most shops are live within one working day. We handle the connection; you just carry on running your store.
Is my data secure?
Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Only you and your authorised team can access it. We never share it with third parties.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. Monthly billing, no contract, no exit fee. If RetailGuard is not delivering value, you can walk away at any time.
Do I need to install anything?
No. RetailGuard runs in your browser. No hardware, no software installs, no IT support needed.
