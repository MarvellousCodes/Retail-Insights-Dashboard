Frequently asked questions

What kind of shops does RetailGuard work for?
Any independent convenience store, forecourt, or small supermarket running a standard point-of-sale system. We connect to your existing stock file, no matter what system you use.
How long does setup take?
Most shops are live within a day. We export your product file, load it in, and run the first scan. You see results the same afternoon.
Is my data safe?
Your data stays on a private, encrypted server. Only you can access it. We never share data between shops or with any third party.
Can I try it before committing?
Yes. The free margin scan gives you a real report using your own data. No card required, no strings attached. If it is not useful, just walk away.
Do I need to install any software?
No. RetailGuard runs entirely in your web browser. You log in from any device: your phone, tablet, or the PC behind the counter.
What if I have multiple shops?
The Multi-site plan gives each location its own dashboard and morning report, plus a combined view so you can compare performance across sites.
