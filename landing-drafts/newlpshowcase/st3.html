Common questions

What till systems do you support?
Any system that can export product and transaction data as a CSV or spreadsheet. That covers most modern EPOS systems, including CBE, Keystroke, and ShopKeep.
How long does setup take?
Most shops are live within 48 hours of sending their first data export. There is no hardware to install and nothing changes on your till.
Can RetailGuard change my prices automatically?
Not without your approval. RetailGuard identifies opportunities and lets you decide. The write-back feature (rolling out on Multi-site) pushes only changes you explicitly confirm.
Is my data secure?
Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Each shop has its own isolated database. We never share data between customers or with third parties.
What if I want to cancel?
Cancel any time with 30 days notice. No exit fees. Your data export is available on request.
