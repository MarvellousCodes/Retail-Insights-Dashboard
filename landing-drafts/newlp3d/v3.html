Questions

How does the data get from my till to RetailGuard?

We connect to your existing till system (CBE, Posiflex, TGI, and others) and pull a daily export. No hardware, no install on the till itself. Takes about 15 minutes to set up.

Can it change prices on my till?

Not without your say-so. RetailGuard is read-only by default. The write-back feature (rolling out for Multi-site plans) only pushes price changes you explicitly approve, one at a time.

What if I have more than one shop?

The Multi-site plan covers multiple locations under one account. Each shop gets its own dashboard with per-site onboarding included.

Is there a contract?

No. Monthly billing, cancel any time. No setup fee.

How long before I see results?

Most shops identify margin issues within the first week of data. The scanner starts working from day one with your next delivery invoice.
