Frequently asked questions

What do I need to get started?
Just a standard till export file (CSV or similar) from your back office. No new hardware, no installation, no changes to your existing POS system.
Is the free scan really free?
Yes. No card details, no obligation, no follow-up pressure. We scan your export and show you what we find. You decide if it is useful.
How long does setup take?
Under 2 minutes. Export your till data, upload it, and your dashboard is live. No column mapping, no manual product entry.
Can RetailGuard change prices in my system?
The Multi-site plan includes write-back, which is rolling out now. On other plans, RetailGuard is read-only. It shows you what to fix, and you apply the changes yourself.
What POS systems do you support?
Any system that can export sales and product data as a standard file. We have worked with CBE, Henderson, BWG, and many others. If yours can export, we can read it.
Is my data safe?
Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share it with third parties. Each shop's data is completely isolated.
